3. Clot Reduction

The vaccines cause your cells to produce the viral spike protein. One of the main effects of the Spike protein is to induce clotting. This clotting can lead to strokes or heart attacks if a major clot occurs. However the most common occurrence is microclots - which can be very pervasive - restricting blood flow to tissues. This can lead to cell death - effecting brain cells, optic nerves, motor nerves etc.

To help restore blood flow, you can take a baby aspirin which acts as a mild anti-clotting agent. This may help restore enough blood flow to avoid permanent damage to organs.
